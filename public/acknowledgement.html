Special Thanks

For invaluable guidance

Dr. Amol Kharche

We express our sincere and heartfelt gratitude to our Project Guide and Mentor, Dr. Amol Kharche, for the invaluable guidance, continuous support, and constant encouragement provided throughout the development of this project. His profound expertise in chemistry and strong command of quantum chemical principles played a crucial role in shaping the conceptual framework and technical direction of this work. His clear explanations of complex topics helped us strengthen our fundamentals and approach the subject with confidence and clarity. Through his constructive feedback and insightful suggestions, we were able to refine our methodology, improve accuracy, and enhance the overall quality of the project. His emphasis on scientific discipline, ethical research practices, and academic rigor ensured that this project met institutional and university standards. He consistently motivated us to think analytically, explore beyond conventional learning, and apply theoretical knowledge to practical and computational aspects of chemistry. His patience in addressing our doubts, guidance during challenges, and encouragement during critical phases of the project were instrumental in the successful completion of this project. This work stands as a reflection of his dedication to education, commitment to student development, and passion for chemistry, and we consider ourselves fortunate to have completed this project under his esteemed mentorship.
