Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our tutorials and learning resources

Are these tutorials free to access?

Yes, all tutorials are completely free to access. We believe in open education and making chemical knowledge accessible to everyone interested in learning.

Do I need prior chemistry knowledge?

Our tutorials are designed for various levels. Beginner tutorials assume no prior knowledge, while advanced tutorials build on fundamental concepts. Check the difficulty level on each tutorial card.

Can I download tutorial materials?

Currently, tutorials are available online only. However, we provide downloadable reference materials, cheat sheets, and exercise files within each tutorial.

How often are new tutorials added?

We add new tutorials monthly. Subscribe to our newsletter to receive updates about new content and learning resources.
